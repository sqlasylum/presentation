Note: A PG16 standby cannot replicate from a PG15 primary via streaming WAL. Use pg_basebackup from server-b (PG15) to seed server-a as a PG15-compatible base, OR skip this phase and go straight to Phase 4 (upgrade server-b now). If uptime is critical, consider using logical replication as a bridge between versions.
